LEASED CIRCUITS

  • A least line is a private telecommunications circuits between two or more locations provided according to a commercial contract
  • It is sometimes also known as a private circuit, and as a data line in the UK
  • These are used by business to connect geographically distant offices

  • Using a dial-up line to transmit data is similar to using the telephone to make a call

    BROADBAND INTERNET ACCESS

  • In telecommunications, broadband is wide bandwidth transport multiple signals and traffic types. The medium can be coaxial cable , optical fiber, radio or twisted pair.


  • in the context of internet access , broadband is used to mean any high-speed internet access that is always on and faster than dial-up access over traditional analog or ISDN PSTN services


    • WHY WE NEED BROADBAND SERVICES

    Eliminates long waiting periods while data is transferred

    Makes work related tasks similar to takes at office via LAN

    Moving large documents via e-mail FLP ,and other means quickly

    Online gaming is more immersive experience and more realistic

    WIRELESS ACCESS METHOD

      SATELLITE CONNECTION

  • Internet access provided through satellite
  • Data is being sent from the satellite to a user's equipment and then translated and decoded
  • Delivered through satellite dish
  • Upload speed-128kbps
  • Download speed-400kbps
  • Satellite internet provides has a FAP(Fair Access Policy)

      • WI-FI(Wireless Fidelity)

  • Big internet buzz word & growing very rapidly
  • Vic Hayes has been named as father of WI-FI
  • Usually requires the ISP to invest a bit in transceivers
  • At least 7 or more end users can be sharing the same master transceiver & still each be able to get up to 1544kbps
  • Practical range is about 90 meters(300 feet) from the transceivers using normal powered transmitters

    • ADVANTAGES AND DIADVANTAGES OF WI-FI

      ADVANTAGES
    • Password and security
    • Eliminate re-configuring computers
    • Avoid deterioration with time
    • Flexible working
      DISADVANTAGES
    • Signal strength sensitivity
    • Climatic conditions

      WLAN

  • Wireless Local Area Network
  • Provide wireless network communication over short distances
  • Users radio or infrared signals instead of traditional network cabling
  • Built by attaching a device called the access point
  • IEEE 802.11 is the foremost standards for wireless LANs
